HITAM (Hyderabad Institute of Technology and Management) is a top B.Tech college in Hyderabad with over 24 years of excellence in engineering education.

HITAM is located in Gowdavelly, Medchal, Telangana, India.

HITAM follows a "Doing Engineering" approach focusing on practical, hands-on learning and industry readiness.

The vision of HITAM is to be a role model technological university of national repute that imparts research-based multidisciplinary competencies.

The mission of HITAM includes building student competencies, collaborating with industries, developing employability skills, and adopting NEP 2020.

HITAM values include integrity, continuous learning, social consciousness, student-centricity, accountability, and teamwork.

HITAM offers B.Tech programs in departments like CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical Engineering, AI & ML, and Data Science.

HITAM has Centres of Excellence in IoT, AI & ML, Robotics, AR/VR, Cyber Security, and VLSI.

HITAM provides research and development programs, incubation centers, and supports student-led startups.

HITAM has NAAC A+ accreditation, NBA accreditation for multiple branches, and UGC Autonomous status.

HITAM ranked in the 101-150 band among privately funded institutions in NIRF.

HITAM collaborates with global universities like MSOE (USA), UAH (USA), RWTH (Germany), and George Mason University.

HITAM campus provides high-quality learning, inclusive environment, labs, sports, and infrastructure.

HITAM was established in 2001 and relocated near Medchal in 2007.

The founder and chairman of HITAM is Arutla Prashanth.
